Strategic Land & Subsidized Housing Acquisitions
FuchsMonte Holding LLC specializes in sourcing, acquiring, and structuring real estate opportunities across high-growth land markets and stable long-term subsidized housing markets. Our focus is on disciplined acquisitions, strong market research, and building long-term real estate value.
About Us
Built Around Disciplined Acquisitions
FuchsMonte is a real estate acquisition company built around finding undervalued opportunities, working with sellers, builders, investors, and operators, and creating value through smart deal sourcing and acquisition strategy.

We work directly with motivated sellers, real estate agents, wholesalers, builders, and institutional buyers to identify and close on properties that meet our strict acquisition criteria. Every deal we pursue is evaluated on its fundamentals — market strength, cash-flow potential, and long-term exit strategy.

Whether we're sourcing vacant lots for residential development or acquiring income-producing rental homes, our approach is the same: move with precision, communicate clearly, and create value for everyone at the table.

Our Services
Two Core Acquisition Strategies
We operate two distinct acquisition verticals, each with its own criteria, markets, and team — giving us deep expertise in both land and residential income-producing real estate.
🏗
Land Acquisition
We acquire and wholesale vacant residential and development land in strong-growth markets. Our team focuses on finding land that matches builder and buyer criteria, including zoning, utilities, flood zones, lot size, access, and development potential. We operate across six high-growth states with active builder networks and strong buyer relationships.
🏠
Subsidized Housing Acquisitions
We acquire income-producing residential properties in subsidized housing markets with a focus on long-term holds, consistent cash flow, and portfolio growth. Our strategy is centered around stable rental demand, affordable housing needs, and long-term ownership. We target markets with strong occupancy fundamentals and Section 8 / HCV voucher activity.
